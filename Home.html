This website is about IMPACT OF ONLINE VIDEO GAMES

The main intention of the website is to tell about the issues that the youth are facing with the online games.

They are totally 4 icons on screen

1. By opening BENEFITS icon, users can see about the advantages of online video games

2. By opening DRAWBACKS icon, users can see about the disadvantages of online video games

3. ADDICTION icon ,describes how actually the youth are getting addicted to online video games

4. DEADDICTION icon tells about the solutions to get rid from online video games
